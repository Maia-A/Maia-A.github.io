Memes

Meme /mēm/ Definition: A humorous image, video, piece of text, etc., that is copied (often with slight variations) and spread rapidly by Internet users. Origin: From Ancient Greek μίμημα (pronounced mīmēma), "imitated thing", from μιμεῖσθαι (mimeisthai), "to imitate", from μῖμος (mimos), "mime"
